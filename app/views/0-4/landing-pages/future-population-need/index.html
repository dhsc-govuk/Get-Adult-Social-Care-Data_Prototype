1. Introduction

Understanding future population needs for adult social care services helps identify emerging mismatches between need and provision.

Here you can explore projections for factors driving local population needs.

For more information on local population needs, check the latest Market Position Statement and Joint Strategic Needs Assessment documents. You will usually find these on the local authority's website.

4. Demographic factors driving population needs

Population needs for adult social care are shaped by a range of factors, including age, income levels, employment status, disability, long-term health conditions, and household composition.

Areas with higher proportions of older adults, people with disabilities, or long-term health conditions typically experience greater pressure on services.
