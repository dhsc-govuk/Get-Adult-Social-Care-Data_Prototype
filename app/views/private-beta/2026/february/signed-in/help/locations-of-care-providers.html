What this measures
All CQC-registered adult social care locations within the local authority, including care home and community based social care providers. Each entry shows the location name, address, service type(s), number of registered beds (care homes only), and latest CQC rating.
Source
Care Directory from the CQC (opens in new tab)
Data correct as of
January 2026
Update frequency
Monthly
Methodology

Locations are drawn from the CQC Care Directory and categorised as follows:

  • Care homes: residential care and nursing care.
  • Community based social care: home care, supported living, extra care housing, and other community based social care (including shared lives services).

These categories are not mutually exclusive - a single location may be registered under multiple service types. Where this is the case, the location appears once with all applicable service types shown.

Each location is allocated to a local authority based on its CQC-registered address.

Limitations

Location of community based services: Community based social care locations are typically registered at the provider's headquarters, which may not be in the same local authority where care is delivered.

Currency of data: Registration information may not be regularly updated and may not reflect services currently provided.

CQC ratings: Some locations may show as "Not Rated" where CQC has not yet carried out an assessment, or where a location provides more than one service type and no longer receives a location-level rating under CQC's current assessment approach. A significant proportion of community based locations do not hold a current rating.

Bed numbers: Registered beds reflect maximum capacity, not necessarily beds currently in operation or occupied.
